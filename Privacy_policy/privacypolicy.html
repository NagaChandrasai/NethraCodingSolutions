Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

At Nethra Coding Solutions, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you visit our website, apply for internships, or use our services.

1. Types of User Information We Collect

We and our service providers collect information when users visit our website, apply for internships, enroll in training programs, or create an account. We maintain two types of user accounts:

  • Student Users – Learners applying for internships, training, or projects.
  • Educator/Partner Users – Mentors or institutions collaborating with us.

When creating accounts, users may provide details such as name, email, phone number, city, state, educational background, and career interests. Payment information (if any) is securely processed by third-party payment gateways like Razorpay or Stripe. We do not store credit card or banking data.

2. How We Use User Information

  • To create and manage user accounts and link learners with mentors.
  • To match users with relevant internships, projects, and training opportunities.
  • To process payments and issue invoices.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To send updates, progress reports, and marketing communications (opt-out available).
  • To improve services, analyze usage, and enhance security.

3. How We Disclose User Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. However, we may share limited data with trusted service providers for hosting, analytics, or payment processing, or when required by law.

4. How We Protect User Information

We use SSL encryption, secure servers, firewalls, and role-based access controls to protect user data. Authorized staff are trained in data security and privacy practices. However, users are responsible for keeping their login credentials safe.

5. Users’ Rights Regarding Their Information

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.
  • Contact us to exercise your rights or for privacy concerns.

6. Storage and Retention of User Information

User data is stored on secure servers in India and the United States. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with laws, or resolve disputes. Inactive accounts may be deleted after extended inactivity.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date. Major changes will be communicated via email or notification.
